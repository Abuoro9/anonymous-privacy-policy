# anonymous-privacy-policy

          

                  'Last Updated: May 2026

                  '1. Overview
                  'Anonymous is designed to provide temporary and privacy-focused anonymous communication. The app allows users to create private chat rooms and temporary public vibes that disappear automatically after a short period.

                  '2. Data Collection
                  'Anonymous does not require account registration, email addresses, phone numbers, or real names.
                  'The app temporarily processes limited technical identifiers such as device-generated IDs and temporary room codes solely for app functionality, spam prevention, realtime communication, and security purposes.
                  'No personal identity information is collected or sold.

                  '3. Messages and Temporary Content
                  'Messages sent within chat rooms are temporary and automatically disappear after a short duration.
                  'Vibes (temporary image or caption posts) are automatically deleted after approximately 30 minutes.
                  'Chat requests and temporary room activity may also be automatically removed after expiration or inactivity.

                  '4. Message Security
                  'Communications inside the app are intended to be private and temporary. The app uses realtime communication technology and security measures designed to reduce long-term storage of user conversations.

                  '5. Local Device Storage
                  'The app may locally store:
                  '- Temporary room history
                  '- Device-generated identifiers
                  '- Basic app preferences
                  'This information remains on your device and is not used for advertising purposes.

                  '6. Vibes Feature
                  'Users may upload temporary vibe images and captions visible to other anonymous users for a limited time.
                  'Users can:
                  '- Create their own vibes
                  '- Delete their own vibes
                  '- Send temporary chat requests through vibes
                  'Users cannot modify or delete other users’ vibes or requests.

                  '7. Anti-Spam and Abuse Prevention
                  'To maintain platform stability and reduce abuse, SecureChat may temporarily limit duplicate requests, excessive activity, or simultaneous anonymous chat sessions.

                  '8. Third-Party Services
                  'Anonymous uses secure backend infrastructure and cloud services necessary for realtime communication, temporary media storage, and app functionality.

                  '9. Children’s Privacy
                  'Anonymous is not directed toward children under the age required by local digital consent laws.

                  '10. Policy Changes
                  'This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect app improvements, security enhancements, or legal requirements.

                  '11. Contact
                  'For questions or privacy concerns, please contact us through the app store listing.',
